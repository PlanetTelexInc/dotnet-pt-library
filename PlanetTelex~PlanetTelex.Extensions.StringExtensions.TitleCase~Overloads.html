StringExtensions.TitleCase Method

Creates a copy of this string that is titled cased. This means that most words in the given string will be all lowercase except for their first letter, which will be a capital. Expections to this rule include a list of words to lowercase when not the first or last word (and, of, etc.). This list is an assembly resource. There is another resource that specifies words to be specifically cased (like iPhone). The items in this resource are supplimented by a provided list of other words in which the casing doesn't follow the general rule.
